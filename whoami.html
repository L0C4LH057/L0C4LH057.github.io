Cybersecurity & Offensive Research

With a deep foundation in penetration testing and bug bounty hunting, I specialize in web application security, Linux exploitation, and Active Directory analysis. Having identified and reported high-impact vulnerabilities for major organizations including Fortune 500 companies I translate complex security flaws into actionable infrastructure hardening. My mission is to push the boundaries of offensive security through rigorous research and the development of automated vulnerability discovery tools.

AI Engineering & Agentic Systems

Bridging five years of experience in AI and machine learning, I move beyond traditional development to create custom deep learning systems and, currently, a Cognitive Operating System (COS). My focus is on the architecture of Agentic AI using frameworks like LangGraph and LangChain, fine-tuning open-weights models like Gemma, and mastering "Context Engineering." I am driven by the potential of AI to automate reasoning and solve complex, real-world problems.

Whether I am mentoring the next generation of ethical hackers or architecting autonomous systems at Brainiacs Innovation and CyberNexus Digital Innovations, I am committed to evolving the standard of security in an increasingly intelligent world.

Consulting & Collaboration

I offer specialized consulting for startups and enterprise teams looking to secure their workloads and integrate intelligent design. My expertise spans security assessments, AI-driven threat detection, and the implementation of robust, machine-learning-powered infrastructure.
