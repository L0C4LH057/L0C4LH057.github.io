About Me

As a dedicated Cybersecurity Researcher, Bug Bounty Hunter, and Ethical Hacker, I specialize in web application security, automation, and Linux exploitation. My work involves identifying and reporting critical vulnerabilities for leading global companies, including Fortune 500s like Netflix and Hilton, where I've uncovered significant misconfigurations, sensitive data exposures, and internal infrastructure leaks.

With over four years of hands-on experience, I have submitted dozens of impactful reports on platforms like HackerOne and Bugcrowd. I am passionate about building, breaking, and teaching security concepts. When I'm not hunting for bugs, I am committed to training the next generation of ethical hackers through intensive bootcamps and community-driven initiatives.

Seeking security expertise? I provide expert consulting services in security assessments and strategic implementation. I am also available to advise high-velocity startups that require a passionate, well-networked technical advisor.
